Privacy Policy

UnitSmart is built with your privacy as a first principle.

Last updated: March 26, 2026

🔒 Short version: UnitSmart does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your device. There are no accounts, no analytics, no ads, and no third-party data sharing.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how UnitSmart ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. UnitSmart is a fully offline, serverless application — there is no backend server, no user accounts, and no internet connection required to use the app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Payment or financial information
  • Contacts, photos, or any other device content

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

All data you enter into UnitSmart (such as product names, prices, and quantities) is stored locally on your device using an on-device SQLite database. This data:

  • Never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it
  • Is not accessible to us or any third party
  • Is permanently deleted when you uninstall the app

4. Data Export / Backup

UnitSmart offers an optional local backup feature that lets you export your comparison data as a JSON file to your device's local storage. This export is initiated entirely by you. We do not receive, store, or have access to any exported data.

5. Notifications

UnitSmart may request permission to send you local notifications (e.g., a monthly reminder to use the app). These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use push notification services, and no data is sent to any server to deliver these notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your iPhone's Settings.

6. Third-Party Services

UnitSmart does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app does not contain any third-party SDKs that collect user data.

7. Children's Privacy

UnitSmart does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our Support page.
